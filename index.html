Blueberry pancake smoothie

requirements needed-1 cup unsweetened almond milk, 1 medium banana, 1/2 cup frozen unsweetened blueberries, 1/4 cup instant plain oatmeal, 1 teaspoon maple syrup, 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon, Dash sea salt

Recipe

Place the first 6 ingredients in a blender; cover and process until smooth. Pour into 2 chilled glasses; sprinkle with sea salt. Serve immediately. 153 calories, 3g fat, 31g carbohydrate, 3g protein.
Disclaimer: Never leave a child unattended in the kitchen. Limit the child to tasks that are safe and age-appropriate.
